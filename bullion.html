About Bullion

Bullion is a multi-stock strategy scanner designed to eliminate manual chart analysis. Users create custom stock lists, select technical strategies such as Shooting Star, and run parallel scans to identify valid trade setups.

Core Capabilities

Parallel Stock Scanning

Analyze multiple stocks simultaneously instead of one chart at a time.

Strategy-Based Filtering

Apply predefined trading strategies to detect valid market patterns.

Buy / SL / Target Levels

Automatically calculate actionable trade levels for eligible stocks.

Custom Stock Lists

Create and reuse personalized stock watchlists for faster analysis.
