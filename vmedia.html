About

One player. Local and cloud, unified.

V-Media Player is built using Python and the VLC library, designed to work seamlessly across both modes — locally for downloaded media, and on the cloud for content streamed directly from storage.

Users can upload up to 5GB of media and stream it directly, without ever needing a separate app for local versus cloud playback.
