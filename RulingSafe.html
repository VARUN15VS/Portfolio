Why this app was built

RulingSafe started as a personal problem.

I watched my father ‚Äî a practicing lawyer ‚Äî struggle to manage court rulings using Excel files, scattered folders, and browser bookmarks. Over time, it became difficult to track documents, links, and case history in one place.

Many legal professionals face the same issue, especially those from non-technical backgrounds. Existing software was either expensive, cloud-dependent, or unnecessarily complex.

So I built RulingSafe: a simple, offline tool that works the way legal professionals already think. One case, one place, everything together.

This application is now being made free and publicly available for anyone who faces similar problems.
