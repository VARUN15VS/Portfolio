Origin

Why RulingSafe exists

RulingSafe started from watching a problem up close.

My father is a practicing lawyer. For years, I watched him manage court rulings the same way most do — a tangle of Excel sheets, scattered folders, printed pages, and browser bookmarks that kept growing and getting harder to navigate. Finding a document from three months ago could mean a 20-minute search before a hearing.

Existing software was either too expensive to justify, required cloud accounts that raised privacy concerns, or was so technically complex that a non-technical user simply wouldn't adopt it. None of it felt like it was made for lawyers — it felt like it was made for IT teams.

So I built RulingSafe to solve exactly that problem: a desktop-first, offline-first, genuinely simple tool that organizes legal work the way a good clerk would — one case, one place, everything together. No learning curve. No cloud. No cost.

It's now free and publicly available for any legal professional who recognizes that problem.
