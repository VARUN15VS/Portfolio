My Projects

CONO-AI: Smart and Privacy focused AI Assistant

Cono AI is an intelligent voice assistant crafted to make everyday computing smarter, faster, and more private. As the Head of AI & Software Development, I’ve helped shape Cono AI into a multifunctional assistant that not only answers general queries and coding questions but also supports real-world use cases like opening WhatsApp, playing media, and managing tasks — all without compromising user data.
What sets Cono AI apart is its commitment to user privacy. All actions are performed locally, with no data being stored on the server or user device. The assistant also features a built-in marketplace and entertainment zone, offering a personalized digital experience.
Built using Python, machine learning models, and custom GUI frameworks, Cono AI is a result of deep research, innovation, and a desire to make AI useful and accessible for everyone. It’s not just a project — it’s a vision to bring human-like intelligence into everyday interactions.

V-Media Player: Advance and Unique feature-rich

V-Media Player is a versatile and secure media player designed to support both local and online streaming, with a special focus on privacy and user personalization. Developed using Python (Tkinter, CustomTkinter) and VLC, this player offers a clean, intuitive interface and a seamless playback experience.
One of its standout features is the 5GB free cloud storage per user, allowing users to stream content directly from a central server. But what truly makes V-Media Player unique is its timestamp system — users can mark, name, and revisit their favorite scenes while watching any media. These timestamps are stored locally in a JSON file, ensuring full control and privacy.
From smooth video playback to intelligent scene tracking, the player is built to give users more than just entertainment — it offers a personal connection to the content they love. Whether you're watching a film or streaming educational videos, V-Media Player transforms your viewing experience with smart features that remember what you value most.

DB-Saver: Database BackUp and Restore Application

DB Saver is a powerful cross-platform application designed to backup and restore databases seamlessly across devices. Built with portability and ease-of-use in mind, it supports more than six major database systems, including MySQL, SQLite, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, MariaDB, OracleDB, and SQL Server.
Whether you're migrating data to a new system, safeguarding against accidental loss, or syncing environments, DB Saver ensures fast, secure, and reliable database transfers. With a user-friendly GUI built using Python and CustomTkinter, even non-technical users can create backups and restore databases with just a few clicks.
What makes DB Saver stand out is its cross-device compatibility — backups can be transferred and restored on entirely different machines, regardless of operating system. It also offers smart detection of database types, auto-path suggestions, and error handling to minimize failure risks.
Designed for developers, DB admins, and enterprises alike, DB Saver is your go-to solution for smart database management in multi-environment setups.
